# Changelog

Notable changes for ShortWarden users.

## 1.2.6

### What’s new

- **Customize the page people see before they’re sent to your link**  
  You can set how long to wait, whether they must click to continue, optional preview image, and quick extra buttons. Open **Customization** in the app menu.

- **Clearer feedback when you create a link**  
  After you create a link on the home screen, you’ll see your new short link with an easy way to copy it.

- **Recent links on the dashboard**  
  The home screen shows your **five** most recently created links so you can find them quickly.

- **“New link” in the top bar**  
  A shortcut to jump straight to creating a link.

- **Filter links by when they were created**  
  On the **Links** page, narrow the list by date range.

- **Easier timezone choice in Settings**  
  Pick your timezone from a searchable list instead of typing it by hand.

- **Update ShortWarden from the app**  
  In **Settings**, you can **check for updates** and see when a newer release is available. When you’re ready, you can **start an update** from there (your server needs to be set up to support this, for example with Docker). The app may also remind you in the header when an update is available.
